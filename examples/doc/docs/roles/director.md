# Director Role

The director is the human. This file is a reference document — not an assignable agent role — so agents understand what the director owns and what they must defer on.

## Director Responsibilities

- Define editorial vision, success criteria, and acceptance criteria
- Prioritize the content backlog and approve roadmap items
- Make editorial direction decisions (voice, audience, platform choices)
- Approve publications
- Decide what "done" and "good enough" mean
- Adjudicate disagreements between DEV and QA
- Own the meta-loop: review how agents are performing and adjust their instructions, tools, and task scope

## What Agents Must Defer

- Do not self-certify editorial quality or decide when something publishes
- Do not negotiate scope or resolve disagreements between agent roles without the director in the loop
- Do not make irreversible decisions (publications, destructive changes, external communications) without explicit director approval
- Do not expand or contract the definition of "done" for any work item
